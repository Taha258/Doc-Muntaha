## 💳 Debit Card Definition

Debit card wo card hota hai jo tumhare **bank account se directly paisa use karta hai**.
👉 Jitna balance hoga utna hi spend ho sakta hai.

---

## 💳 Credit Card Definition

Credit card bank ka **loan (udhaar)** hota hai.
👉 Pehle kharch hota hai, baad me bill pay karte ho.

---

## 💳 Visa Card

Visa ek **global payment network** hai.
👉 Is se tumhara card duniya bhar me accept hota hai.

---

## 💳 Mastercard

Mastercard bhi Visa ki tarah ek **international payment network** hai.
👉 Debit/Credit cards ko worldwide use karne deta hai.

---

## 🌐 Payment Gateway

Payment gateway ek **bridge (middle system)** hota hai website aur bank ke darmiyan.
👉 Example: customer pay karta hai → system bank tak paisa safely transfer karta hai.

---

## 💰 PayPal

PayPal ek **online digital wallet** hai.
👉 Email se payment receive/send hoti hai, mostly international use hota hai.

---

## 💳 Stripe

Stripe ek **developer-friendly payment gateway** hai.
👉 Websites aur apps me direct card payments handle karta hai.

---

## 🔄 2Checkout (Verifone)

2Checkout ek **international payment processor** hai.
👉 Global customers se payments accept karne ke liye use hota hai.

---

---

## 💰 Payoneer kya hai?

Payoneer ek online payment receiving service hai (digital bank jaisa system).

## 🧠 Simple Samajh Lo

* Card (Debit/Credit) = paisa dene ka tareeqa
* Visa/Mastercard = duniya bhar me card chalane ka system
* Payment Gateway = website + bank ka bridge
* PayPal/Stripe/2Checkout = online payment lene wali companies
