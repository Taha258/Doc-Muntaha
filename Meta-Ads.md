# D2C Ecommerce Marketing Metrics (India)

| Metric | Meaning / Why It Matters | Formula | Ideal Value (India D2C) |
|---|---|---|---|
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | Measures how engaging your ad is. Low CTR = bad creative or audience. | (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100 | Meta: 1.5–3%<br>Google: 3–6% |
| CPC (Cost per Click) | How much you pay per visitor from ads. | Spend ÷ Clicks | Meta: ₹3–₹12<br>Search: ₹5–₹25 |
| Landing Page View Rate | Checks if users actually reach your website after clicking. | (Landing Page Views ÷ Link Clicks) × 100 | >85% |
| Bounce Rate | % of users leaving after first page. | (Single Page Sessions ÷ Total Sessions) × 100 | <55% |
| Average Session Duration | Average time spent on your site. | From GA4 directly | >45 sec |
| Pages per Session | Measures how many pages an average visitor explores. | Pageviews ÷ Sessions | 2–3 pages |
| CPM (Cost per 1,000 Impressions) | How much you pay to reach 1,000 people. Indicates audience cost & creative quality. | (Spend ÷ Impressions) × 1000 | Meta: ₹80–₹150<br>YouTube: ₹40–₹100 |
| Add to Cart (ATC) Rate | Measures user interest in the product. | (ATC ÷ LP Views) × 100 | 8–12% of LP Views |
| Initiate Checkout Rate | Measures how many ATCs move to checkout. | (Initiate Checkouts ÷ ATC) × 100 | 40–50% of ATCs |
| Purchase Conversion Rate (CVR) | % of visitors who purchase after clicking. | (Purchases ÷ Clicks) × 100 | Meta: 2–4%<br>Google: 3–6% |
| Cost per Add to Cart (CPATC) | How much you pay for each product added to cart. | Spend ÷ ATCs | ₹25–₹50 |
| Cost per Purchase (CPP) | Cost to generate one sale. | Spend ÷ Purchases | ₹150–₹400 |
| ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) | Shows how much revenue you earn per ₹1 spent. | Revenue ÷ Spend | 3x+ (breakeven)<br>4–6x (profit zone) |
| Frequency | Average number of times a person saw your ad. | Impressions ÷ Reach | Cold: <2.5<br>Warm: 2.5–5 |
| Ad Engagement Rate | Engagement (likes, saves, shares) relative to impressions. | (Engagements ÷ Impressions) × 100 | >4% |
| Quality Ranking (Meta) | Meta’s score for relevance & engagement quality. | From Ads Manager | Above Average |
| View Content → ATC Ratio | Tells if product offer & price appeal to users. | (ATC ÷ View Content) × 100 | >25–30% |
| Impression Share | Your visibility compared to competition. | (Impressions ÷ Eligible Impressions) × 100 | Search: >60%<br>Display: >40% |
| Search Lost IS (Budget) | % of times ads didn’t show due to low budget. | From Google Ads competitive metrics | <10% |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Average revenue per order. | Total Revenue ÷ Total Orders | ₹900–₹1500 |
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | Cost to acquire one new customer. | Total Spend ÷ New Customers | <⅓ of AOV |
| Lifetime Value (LTV) | Revenue per customer over time. | Total Customer Revenue ÷ No. of Purchases | >3× CAC |
| Repeat Purchase Rate | % of returning buyers. | (Repeat Customers ÷ Total Customers) × 100 | ~25% |
| Refund / Return Rate | % of refunded or returned orders. | (Refunded Orders ÷ Total Orders) × 100 | <10% |
| Blended ROAS (All Channels) | Combined ROAS from Meta, Google, and organic. | Total Revenue ÷ Total Ad Spend (All Channels) | 3.5x+ |
| Overall Site Conversion Rate | Conversion rate of entire website traffic. | (Total Purchases ÷ Total Sessions) × 100 | 2–3% |
