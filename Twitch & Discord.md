# Monetization Requirements on Twitch

Twitch par affiliate banne ke liye usually:

* 50 followers
* 500 total minutes streamed
* 7 different stream days
* 3 average viewers

phir:

* Subs
* Bits
* Donations
* Ads
* Sponsorships

se earning hoti hai.
